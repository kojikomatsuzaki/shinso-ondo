# English

This folder contains the official audio recordings of **Shinso Ondo**.

The collection currently consists of the official master recordings. Additional officially released versions and related recordings may be added in the future.

## Contents

| File | Description |
|------|-------------|
| `shinso-ondo-shinsokai-shinsoelem.wav` | Official master recording performed by members of **Shinsokai** and volunteer students of **Shinso Elementary School** |
| `shinso-ondo-hanawa-aki.wav` | Official master recording performed by Japanese folk singer **Aki Hanawa** |

---

## Audio Specifications

The files currently published are the original master recordings used for official distribution.

Audio formats may vary depending on the material.
