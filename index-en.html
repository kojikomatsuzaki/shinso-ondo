Shinso Ondo

新荘音頭

日本語

Shinso Ondo is a community dance song created for the Shinso Summer Festival in the Shinso district of Mito, Ibaraki, Japan.

This website provides official audio recordings, lyrics, musical scores, publications, structured metadata, and rights and license information.
